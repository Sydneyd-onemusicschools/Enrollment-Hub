Instrument Rentals
We offer Instrument Rentals! If someone wants to rent an instrument, forward the call to the studio or have them stop by. With questions reach out to Yvonne.
  • Bring your own instrument for band or string lessons.
  • Need a rental? Contact our office.
  • We rent most band/orchestral strings through Music & Arts — no guitar or piano rentals.
  • With notice, we can loan a guitar or other instrument for the first lesson only.
  • You’ll need your own instrument at home to practice.
